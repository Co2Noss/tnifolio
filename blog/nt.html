Calls, Sales, and Note Templates. Oh My!

The Note Template has come a long way since its inception back in April of 2016. Every iteration was cared for by multiple suggestions from call takers like myself looking to make their job easier. The image you see above is an early concept art and design for the note template. I spent lots of time designing it to look exactly how I wanted. I've even uploaded a copy for you to check out. You'll notice it's missing a lot of the features we've come to depend on now. Copy paste is gone, reset is gone, and there's no output!

I eventually summoned the courage to ask a good friend of mine to assist me with this project. He agreed with the notion that I would not share it with anyone else because neither of us were being paid for it. It began with writing up a beta.html mock up and some quick code. We called it; ProjectLantern. You'll notice quite a few features that are pre-filled out or have changed dramatically since then. The 'Approved By' field was pre-set with the L2s on my current team. I had 0 plans to distribute and share my application so there wasn't much need to allow user input for many things. The biggest hurdle from the first beta was that regardless of whether or not you had filled out the field it output anyway. What a mess.

As I started to develop this template it went through several updates and eventually caught my TLs ear that I had developed an easier way to take notes during calls. I was asked to provide a link to the template and demonstrate it to a Level 2 on my team Charles "Brad" Atkinson. They saw no issues with me using it and I was even allowed to start sending out team emails to see if anyone else was interested in using it. This was the first example of the note template gaining recognition, but it was certainly not the last time....
